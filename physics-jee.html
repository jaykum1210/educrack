What is This Course About?

Physics is everywhere! From kicking a football to soaring airplanes , everything follows the laws of physics.

This course will guide you on an exciting journey to understand, explore, and master physics in a fun and simple way.

We’ll break down tough topics into easy concepts using real-life examples, fun visuals, and smart problem-solving tricks !
